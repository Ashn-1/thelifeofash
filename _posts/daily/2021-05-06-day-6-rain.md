> I love rain  
Not the stormy kind  
The pouring kind  
Straight down from heaven  
>
> Rain  
Wash away the sorrow  
The hurt, the pain  
And cleanse your soul  
>
> My head is cold  
My shoes are soaking  
My mind is calm  
The rain is pouring
